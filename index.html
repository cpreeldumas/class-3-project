NYC's Highest Emission Housing

This map shows the latest data on NYC's 30 highest polluting multifamily housing buildings, as measured by the total greenhouse gases emitted due to energy used by the property per gross square foot (kgCO2e/ft2).

Each marker is color-coded by the building's subsidized housing status. As the city works to decarbonize its housing stock, this map can help direct resources to high-emission buildings and identify publicly-funded housing that qualifies for additional city retrofitting and loan programs.
